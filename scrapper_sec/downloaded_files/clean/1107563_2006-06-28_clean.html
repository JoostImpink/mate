transaction whereby the issuer acquired all of the outstanding capital stock of sinoenergy and the former stockholders of sinoenergy acquired a controlling interest in the issuer in a transaction accounted for as a reverse acquisition. this acquisition and related transactions are described in the issuer's form 8-k with a report date of june 2, 2006 and a filing date of june 15, 2006 (the
