resales of issuer common stock issued to the stockholders in the acquisition, subject to certain limitations (the “resale shelf”) and to cause the resale shelf to become effective no later than the expiration of the lock-up period. the investor rights agreement also prohibits the stockholders from acquiring additional voting securities of the issuer without the issuer’s consent, or otherwise
