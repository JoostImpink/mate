large refined petroleum product marketing companies and major end users of refined petroleum products on a fee basis through facilities it owns and operates; the storage of natural gas; the marketing chemical companies. the partnership’s general partner is the general partner. the general partner is a limited liability company organized under the laws of delaware. the general partner’s principal
