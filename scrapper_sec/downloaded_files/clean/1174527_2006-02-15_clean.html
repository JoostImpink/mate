financing, and the corresponding debt obligation will remain with abc chicago. merger agreement pursuant to the merger agreement, and subject to the terms and conditions set forth therein, immediately subsidiary of citadel. at the closing of the merger, each share of abc chicago common stock will be automatically converted into the right to receive one fully paid and nonassessable share of common of citadel holding the remaining approximately 48%. prior to the closing of the merger and pursuant to the merger agreement, citadel will declare a special dividend payable immediately prior to the closing of the merger to holders of common stock of citadel of record at a date to be set prior to the closing of the merger. the amount of the dividend will be determined based on the market price of
