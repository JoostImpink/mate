henckels were appointed to the board of directors of the issuer on november 10, 2011.  pursuant to the terms of the investor rights agreement, alara capital’s director candidates will also be nominated for election at the issuer’s 2012 annual meeting of shareholders.               cusip no. 375175106 schedule 13d page 7 of 10     the investor rights agreement also provides, subject to the
