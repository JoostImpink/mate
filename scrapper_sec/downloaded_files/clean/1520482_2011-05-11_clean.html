exchange agreement (the “exchange agreement”) by and among the issuer, fortune health management limited (“fortune health”), and the holders of all outstanding shares of fortune health (the “fortune health shareholders”), including the bvi holding companies, the issuer acquired all of the outstanding shares of fortune health (the “fortune health shares”) from the fortune health shareholders, and to increase its authorized ordinary shares. together, the 19,400,325 ordinary shares and the 135,802,275 additional ordinary shares total 155,202,600 ordinary shares, or 96% of the issuer’s ordinary shares on a fully-diluted basis.   item 4.   purpose of transaction.   prior to the share exchange, the issuer was a “shell company” (as such term is defined in rule 12b-2 under the securities the business of franchising and operating foot massage spas in the prc. pursuant to the exchange agreement, and giving effect to the additional 97,042,043 ordinary shares that the issuer is obligated
