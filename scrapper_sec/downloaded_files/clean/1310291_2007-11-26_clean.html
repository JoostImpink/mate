1.     security and issuer . this statement relates to shares of common stock, $.001 par value (the “common stock”), of axiom iii, inc., a nevada corporation  (the “issuer”). at present, there are 50,000,000 issued and outstanding shares of common stock, and a total of 50,000,000 authorized shares of common stock. the reporting persons are mon hung lew, a citizen and resident of the hong kong sar of the people’s republic of china (hereinafter, “mr. lew”), who is the ceo, cfo and sole owner of profit gain management limited,  a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the british virgin islands (“profit gain”). profit gain is also the other reporting person. the reporting persons are each the beneficial owners of 17,405,550 shares of common stock. the issuer’s principal executive offices are located at suite 1606-7, 16/f, great eagle centre, 23 harbour road, hong kong sar of the people’s republic of china. item 2.     identity and background .   a. the names of the reporting persons are mr. lew and profit gain. b.              the business address of mr. lew is suite 1606-7, 16/f, great eagle center, 23 harbour road, hong kong sar of the people’s republic of china.  the registered office of profit gain is also room 1606-7, 16/f, great eagle center, 23 harbour road, hong kong sar of the people’s republic of china.   c. mr. lew’s principal business is acting as a private investor.  mr. lew is also the ceo, cfo and sole owner of profit gain. profit gain’s principal business is that of a holding company for investments made by mr. lew. laws. f.   profit gain is a company organized and existing under the laws of the british virgin islands. item 3.     source and amount of funds or other consideration . pursuant to a share exchange eastern concept consulting. the share exchange agreement was attached as exhibit 2.2 to a form 8-k filed with the commission on november 9, 2007, and is incorporated by reference hereby. eastern concept is a holding company, with audited and pro forma financials presented in the 8-k that was filed on october 24, 2007, whose sole purpose was to effect the reverse merger with axio and later people’s bank of china to collect deposits as prepayment stored in the chip embedded in the cards that it issues. the amount of funds or other consideration that were paid by mr. lew to acquire the cfo and secretary of the issuer in a private transaction. - 4 -     item 4.     purpose of transaction . the purpose of the transactions were to consummate a reverse merger of foshan into the issuer.
