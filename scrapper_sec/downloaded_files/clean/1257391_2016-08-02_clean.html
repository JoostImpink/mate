where wa participated in a secondary offering during a restricted period.  all of these instances were voluntarily disclosed to the sec.  the violations allegedly occurred between january 2011 and june 2012.  rule 105 generally prohibits purchasing an equity security in a registered follow-on public offering if the purchaser sold short the same security during the shorter of the period: (1) or form 1-e and ending with the pricing.   in july 2014, wa voluntarily submitted to an offer of settlement with respect to the five alleged violations of rule 105, without admitting or denying the sec's allegations.  the sec accepted the offer of settlement, and imposed a cease-and-desist order from future violations of rule 105.  the settlement involved the payment by wa of disgorgement of the issuer's new seven member board of directors.  each of wa and one other supporting holder shall retain the right to designate one director nominee for so long as its ownership of outstanding
